I Finally Watched These

Key Points

  • The author is undertaking a multi-year project to watch every video game adaptation movie ever made to rank them from worst to best
  • Copyright issues prevent making full videos on modern movies, so this format covers four films in one discussion
  • 2001's Tomb Raider with Angelina Jolie holds sentimental value - watched after a hospital visit for a dog bite eye injury
  • The 2001 film features excellent action choreography with video game-style moves, back flips, dual wielding, and Matrix-inspired bullet dodging
  • Angelina Jolie wore breast enhancers ("booby boosters") to more closely resemble Lara Croft's exaggerated video game proportions
  • The plot involves the Illuminati seeking a triangle key within a strict planetary alignment timeframe to control time itself
  • Daniel Craig appears as Alex, a money-motivated character working for the bad guys with cringe-worthy dialogue and exposition
  • The film's biggest weakness is its flat story with no emotional weight or character chemistry
  • Cradle of Life (the second Angelina Jolie film) is considered significantly worse in every department, especially visual effects
  • Gerard Butler plays Terry, essentially the same character as Daniel Craig's Alex from the first film
  • The villain in Cradle of Life is a bioweapons expert who wants to use Pandora's Box (containing a plague) to destroy the world
  • 2018's Tomb Raider closely adapts the reboot video game, making a legitimate attempt to translate game content to screen
  • Walton Goggins provides the best villain performance across all Tomb Raider movies with actual motivation - wanting to return to his family
  • The 2018 film shows a weaker, learning Lara Croft at the beginning of her journey, getting beaten in fights initially
  • The movie suffers from "quirkification" - unnecessary quirky dialogue and humor typical of its era
  • Himiko is revealed to be not a magical being, but a disease carrier, with the disease creating zombie-like effects
  • The 2018 film ends with sequel bait revealing Lara's guardian is actually working with the ancient organization
  • An Assassin's Creed movie was also watched but deemed so bad it might warrant its own standalone video
