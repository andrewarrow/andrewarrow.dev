Michael Singer Inspired Guided Meditation - Get Unstuck

Key Points

  • Find a quiet space and close your eyes to begin the meditation
  • Take deep breaths and notice your body from feet to head
  • Bring awareness to your chest as it rises and falls
  • Bring the stuck event or situation into your awareness
  • Ask "what part of me is disturbed by this?" instead of "what should I do?"
  • Honor and respect the situation with openness
  • Replay the event like a screenshot or video in your mind
  • Notice any feelings in your body and where they arise
  • Lean away from judgments and your mind's noise
  • Give room to let negative feelings about the situation pass through you
  • Remember that every moment of life is a spiritual experience
  • You don't have to work out every issue - just relax and be present
  • Let whatever is bothering you pass through you
  • If your heart begins to close, consciously choose not to close
  • Lean away from your mind into silence for clarity
  • Your path forward will become obvious in this state
  • Feel gratitude for the miracle of life and all the moments that brought you here
  • Go in peace and do not let your heart close
