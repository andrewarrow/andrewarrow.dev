Fears Surrounding Possible US Military Action Against Mexican Cartels

Key Points

  • El Chapo's sons are cutting deals and forming alliances with the New Generation Cartel led by "El Mencho" (Nemesio Oseguera Cervantes)
  • Trump has authorized potential military action in Latin America, including Mexico, to combat drug cartels
  • Mexican federal police are compromised - they tipped off cartel leaders about arrest operations
  • US surveillance drones are mapping targets in Mexico, indicating military operations are being planned
  • Mexican military officials warn that US intervention without approval could unite cartels and military against America
  • The Zetas cartel (formed by Fort Bragg-trained former special forces) militarized cartel warfare and introduced torture videos as propaganda
  • Cartels now use advanced drone warfare tactics, dropping bombs and conducting coordinated attacks on military forces
  • Mexican fighters returning from Ukraine are bringing sophisticated drone operation skills back to cartels
  • The New Generation Cartel has absorbed lessons from all previous cartels and represents the most sophisticated criminal organization yet
  • A potential nightmare scenario is emerging where this super-cartel gains direct border access to the United States
  • Border crossings have decreased by 90% but cartels have adapted with expensive new smuggling methods
  • Cartels use fake IDs, fast boats, and wave runners to smuggle people across the border
  • US Navy ships are now positioned off the Mexican coast near Tijuana
  • There's a breakdown in trust between Mexican and US authorities due to widespread corruption
  • Anti-US sentiment in Mexico is being exploited by politicians who blame America for cartel violence
  • The conflict evolved from gang violence to guerrilla warfare after the Zetas introduced military tactics
  • Cartels now possess anti-drone technology and are engaged in drone-vs-drone warfare
  • Mexicans fear both US intervention and the consequences of allowing cartel expansion to continue unchecked
