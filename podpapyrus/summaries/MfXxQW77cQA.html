The World War II Consensus is Breaking Down

Key Points

  • Foundation myths and sacred stories have expiration dates and cannot hold the world together forever
  • World War II serves as the foundational myth for our current geopolitical world and value systems
  • The WWII consensus is breaking down as the last generation with direct memory of the war passes away
  • All foundation myths contain hidden sins or contradictions that create blind spots and taboos
  • The West allied with Stalin (a tyrant) to defeat Hitler, then handed half of Europe to communist control
  • Communist imagery gets a "pass" in Western society while Nazi imagery creates immediate condemnation
  • Normal identity (national, ethnic, gender) has become increasingly associated with tyranny and fascism
  • The breakdown manifests as contradictory movements where even opposing sides participate in dismantling the old consensus
  • Kanye West serves as an "inadvertent mystic" who manifests coming cultural shifts through contradictory behavior
  • The taboo system creates a pattern where anything remotely connected to Nazi imagery becomes completely forbidden
  • This has led to a "rainbow tyranny" or "imposition of openness" that destroys traditional identities
  • The danger lies in naive embrace of the "anti-story" - people thinking Nazis were actually right all along
  • Ancient symbols like the swastika cannot remain permanently captured by their Nazi association
  • The breakdown is happening across politics, geopolitics, and cultural understanding simultaneously
  • Reintegration must happen carefully to avoid releasing "demons" of resentment and counter-narrative extremism
  • Historical precedent exists for reintegrating controversial figures (like Alexander the Great) into broader narratives
  • Those marginalized by the current narrative become tempted to embody the counter-story as "Satanists" to the system
  • A proper synthesis or reintegration is needed rather than simply flipping to the opposite extreme
