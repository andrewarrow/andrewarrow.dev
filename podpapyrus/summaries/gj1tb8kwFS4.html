Haley Gosserand from Building the band talk about Alison and Sweet Seduction drama

Key Points

  • Haley Gossaran appeared on Netflix's new show "Building a Band"
  • Her mother was battling breast cancer during filming, which was the hardest time of her life
  • She was able to be with her mom through chemo treatments and first surgery before filming
  • Her mother encouraged her to do the show despite Haley's initial reluctance to leave her
  • Being 20 years old, alone in the UK without her phone in a high-stress situation was very difficult
  • She opened up to bandmates about her mom's cancer to seek support and build connections
  • She maintained a positive, kind, and empathetic attitude throughout the experience
  • She focused on keeping peace and making things work rather than engaging with negativity
  • She wanted to build a successful band as a team player and help everyone shine together
  • She hoped to form a sisterhood and supportive friendship like other successful bands on the show
  • She observed that successful bands had mutual respect and balance, which her band lacked
  • She continued to show grace after filming, hoping friendship could develop even if the band didn't work
  • Watching the show back and hearing solo interview comments was eye-opening and difficult
  • She realized she had been letting too much slide and eventually needed to protect her peace
  • Her difficult life experiences made her mentally stronger and able to avoid lashing back
  • Her friends and family were proud of how she carried herself despite being upset about her treatment
  • She's grateful for the opportunity and excited about upcoming music, TV, and film projects
  • Her message to others going through difficult times is to never give up and remember that kindness always wins
