Key Points

  • Daddy Jack and Adam from Sons of Legion conducted a social experiment comparing AI-generated music to human-made music
  • They covered a viral song that is suspected to be AI-generated to test if people can tell the difference
  • The experiment aims to gauge reactions and see if people can distinguish between computer-generated and human-created music with "soul"
  • AI music generation now allows users to prompt for songs and receive results in about 30 seconds
  • They conducted street interviews on Broadway in Nashville, the heart of American music, asking people to identify the difference
  • The reactions from people were described as physical, emotional, and sometimes confused
  • This represents a significant moment in music history happening in real-time
  • We are in an unprecedented time where many people cannot tell the difference between AI and human-generated content
  • The recording process involved detailed microphone setup on drums, emphasizing the human artistry in audio engineering
  • Recording engineering is described as an art form with masters who have spent lifetimes perfecting drum sounds since the 1930s-1960s
  • The human touch in engineering is considered important, with dedicated engineers focusing solely on capturing the best sound
  • The iconic drum sounds on famous recordings came from this specialized human expertise
  • The song lyrics include themes of war, peace, and social justice with lines like "Dust on the wind, boots on the ground"
  • The experiment is presented without claiming there's a right or wrong answer - it's purely observational
  • The project involved setting up multiple microphones including guitar mic, room mic, two vocals, toms, high hat, and bass DI
  • The cover song includes themes of resistance with lyrics like "Raise your hand, don't look away, sing out loud, make them pay"
  • The experiment reveals how AI technology is challenging traditional notions of musical authenticity and creativity
  • The project demonstrates the current state of AI music generation technology and its impact on the music industry
