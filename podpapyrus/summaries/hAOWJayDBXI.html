Sandra Moone vs Mazzerati - Future Legends of Women's Wrestling - Doors, Ladders & Chairs Match!

Key Points

  • Sandra Moon defeats Maserati to become the new FSW Women's Champion in a doors, ladders, and chairs match
  • This was a first-time ever doors, ladders, and chairs match for the women's championship at FSW
  • The rivalry between Sandra Moon and Maserati runs deep - they were in the first championship match when the FSW women's title was introduced three years ago
  • Maserati was the first of the two to win the championship originally, making this rivalry personal
  • Both competitors showed complete disregard for their own safety and each other's safety throughout the match
  • Maserati was busted open and bleeding during the match after hitting a chair
  • Multiple weapons were used including steel chairs, doors, and ladders as stipulated in the match type
  • Sandra Moon put her body at risk with high-impact moves and took significant punishment
  • The crowd was firmly behind Sandra Moon, wanting to see her fulfill her dream of winning the title
  • Maserati's cockiness and ego ultimately led to her downfall in the match
  • Sandra Moon had to climb a ladder to retrieve the championship belt to win
  • The match featured extreme violence with both women willing to shorten their careers to defeat each other
  • Sandra Moon spent years training at the FSW Academy with the goal of becoming a professional wrestler
  • There wasn't even a women's division when Sandra Moon first started at FSW
  • Sandra Moon thanked previous champions including Heather, Taio, Lacy, and Maserati in her victory speech
  • She credited her support system including Jody, Adriel, Laz, Chris Mounts, and her family for her success
  • The entire women's locker room celebrated with the new champion after her victory
  • Sandra Moon acknowledged that winning the title puts a target on her back going forward
