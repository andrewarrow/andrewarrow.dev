How to Start a Podcast on YouTube 2025 (Step-by-Step Guide)

Key Points

  • More people watch podcasts on YouTube than any other platform
  • Younger generations find more podcasts on YouTube than anywhere else
  • YouTube is the best way to get your podcast in front of new viewers organically
  • You need to verify your identity in YouTube Studio to upload videos over 15 minutes
  • Profile picture should be 800x800 pixels and channel banner should be 2048x1152 pixels
  • Keep important text and imagery in the center of your channel banner for visibility across platforms
  • Record with high-quality video and audio using local recording on each person's device
  • Share your screen while recording to keep the podcast visually engaging
  • Create a custom thumbnail for every episode in 16:9 aspect ratio (3840x2160 pixels for 4K)
  • Mark one playlist as a podcast to tell YouTube it's a podcast and appear in YouTube Music
  • Upload square podcast artwork (up to 3000 pixels square) for the podcast playlist
  • Be careful not to include copyrighted material like extended media clips or music
  • You can download SRT transcripts and upload them to YouTube for better subtitles
  • Schedule episodes to publish in the morning for the best chance of being discovered
  • Create YouTube Shorts from your episodes to promote your show across platforms
  • Post Shorts consistently (daily if possible) when you don't have full episodes
  • Use vertical 9:16 format with animated captions for Shorts to maximize engagement
  • Add video and image overlays to Shorts to make them visually engaging
