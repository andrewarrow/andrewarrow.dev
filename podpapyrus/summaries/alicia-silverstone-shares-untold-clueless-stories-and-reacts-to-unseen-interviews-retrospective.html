Alicia Silverstone Shares UNTOLD Clueless Stories and Reacts to UNSEEN Interviews | rETrospective

Key Points

  • Alicia Silverstone discusses her ET retrospective covering her career from 1992 onwards
  • She auditioned multiple times for "The Crush" (1993) and was thrilled when she got the role
  • George Clooney was very kind to her during "Batman & Robin" and had a big brother vibe
  • Fred Savage got her phone number but it went to a fax machine, making him think she gave him a fake number
  • She found her people in theater and was interested in the therapeutic aspect of acting
  • The violent scene in "The Crush" where she slaps and gets thrown against a wall was exciting to film
  • Cary Elwes took care of her on "The Crush" set when she was just 15-16 years old
  • She didn't know who Aerosmith was when filming their music video at age 16
  • Steven Tyler and Steven Dorff made strong impressions during the Aerosmith video
  • "Clueless" (1995) became a cultural phenomenon that no one could have predicted
  • "As if" is the most quoted line people say to her from "Clueless"
  • A "Clueless" reboot is in very early stages and she's excited but wants to honor the original
  • She describes Cher as a happy, motherly character who tries to please everyone
  • Ryan O'Neal would introduce himself as "Ryan O'Neal, Love Story" every day on "Miss Match"
  • Bradley Cooper played her boyfriend on "Miss Match" as a metrosexual character
  • Her son Bear is now 14 and she maintains amazing communication with him without grounding
  • She's proud of her Irish mystery show where she plays someone searching for her missing father
  • Her teenage son loves watching her current show and wanted to see episode after episode
