S2E9 - The Bold & The Blasphemous

Key Points

  • Pineapple does not belong on pizza - it just doesn't go together
  • Cilantro is a great herb that tastes good, not like soap, and is essential in Latin cooking and stews
  • Medium well is the preferred steak doneness - not rare (still mooing) and not well done (too tough)
  • Avocados are bland by themselves and only taste good with salt, lemon, or other seasonings
  • Lemon Oreos (the thin ones) are superior to regular chocolate Oreos with vanilla filling
  • Frosting is essential on cake and ice cream should be firm, not soft and melty
  • Biggie had better flow and music than Tupac, despite having a shorter catalog
  • Eminem has impressive technical skills but his music isn't very replayable
  • Kendrick Lamar's music doesn't appeal to everyone, despite being critically acclaimed
  • J. Cole is humble, clever, and authentic - not living a fake lifestyle like other rappers
  • Drake delivers good music regardless of whether he writes his own lyrics
  • Lil Wayne is extremely clever and underrated, with short-lived peak that was unfairly dismissed
  • Good employees burn out because they get punished with more work for performing well
  • People don't leave bad jobs - they leave bad managers and lie about the real reason
  • Open communication is encouraged at work until you say something real or challenging
  • Management promotes people who won't challenge them rather than the most qualified
  • Not everyone finds kids cute and that's acceptable - some kids are just annoying
  • The Chi TV show's episode 12 was terrible with unrealistic plot points and soap opera-like drama
