Interdimensional Beings have visited us, Congresswoman Claims – What might it mean?

Key Points

  • US Congress member claims military officials have seen evidence of interdimensional beings
  • Air Force veteran Anna Luna discussed these claims on Joe Rogan's podcast
  • Unidentified objects reportedly accelerate extremely quickly, faster than current technology allows
  • These objects appear to be intelligently controlled and reactive to human maneuvers
  • Human body can withstand about 9 G forces; F-16 aircraft can handle 17-18 G forces before structural failure
  • Reported UAPs perform in excess of 1,000-3,000 G forces
  • Navy officers used the word "stalked" in secure emails when discussing ships being pursued by UAPs
  • Interdimensional travel implies existence of more than our known 3 spatial dimensions plus time
  • String theory requires 10 total dimensions, though most are supposedly rolled up to small radii
  • Other theories like Eric Weinstein's geometric unity and DGP brane model also propose extra dimensions
  • No evidence exists that these additional dimensions actually exist
  • No known way for normal matter objects to travel in extra dimensions even if they existed
  • Extra dimensions wouldn't allow faster-than-light travel due to speed limits and inertia
  • Simply positing extra dimensions doesn't explain the observed phenomena
  • Advanced propulsion technology or optical illusions are more likely explanations
  • Talk of interdimensional beings may be mix of superstition and desire for attention
  • The holographic principle was mentioned as a framework for understanding multi-dimensionality
  • Interdimensional space travel is similar to fictional hyperdrives, as opposed to warp drives or wormholes
