Bernie RIPS Elon Musk's Efforts To Create "Indentured Servants" Through H-1B Visa Program

Key Points

  • The H-1B program primarily replaces good-paying American jobs with lower-paid guest workers, not hiring "the best and brightest"
  • 85% of H-1B visas go to entry-level and junior workers paid 20-40% less than American workers in similar positions
  • Top 30 H-1B companies laid off 85,000 American workers while bringing in 34,000 guest workers (2022-2023)
  • 33% of all new information technology jobs in America are filled by guest workers
  • Millions of Americans with STEM degrees are not employed in their fields while H-1B workers fill those positions
  • Half of top 30 H-1B employers are "body shops" that specialize in outsourcing jobs
  • Tesla laid off 7,500 American workers while applying for thousands of H-1B workers
  • H-1B workers are employed in non-specialized roles like fashion models, dog trainers, massage therapists, and English teachers
  • Current system treats H-1B workers as indentured servants who can lose visas if they complain about working conditions
  • Amendment would double H-1B fees to fund 25,000 annual scholarships for American STEM students
  • Amendment would raise H-1B wages to at least median local wage to prevent undercutting American workers
  • Amendment would prohibit replacing laid-off American workers with H-1B guest workers
  • Amendment would make H-1B visas portable so workers can change employers
  • Real weekly wages for average American workers are lower today than 50 years ago despite increased productivity
  • $50 trillion wealth transfer occurred from bottom 90% to top 1% over recent decades
  • 60% of Americans live paycheck to paycheck while the country has record wealth inequality
  • Trade agreements like NAFTA eliminated manufacturing jobs with promises of tech jobs that were then filled by guest workers
  • American workers have constitutional rights to unionize and collectively bargain, unlike guest workers
