KITT Introduction | Knight Rider

Key Points

  • The car is called the Knight 2000 and is described as the most expensive car in the world
  • It's the fastest, safest, and strongest car in the world according to Devon
  • The vehicle is completely fuel efficient and operated entirely by microprocessors
  • The car has artificial intelligence - it thinks and can make decisions
  • Its surface is made of a new substance that's bonded at the molecular level, not paint or metal
  • The car is virtually impossible to be involved in mishaps or collisions unless specifically ordered
  • It can automatically steer itself around obstacles when the driver acts contrary to their best interests
  • The car's primary function is the preservation of human life, specifically Michael's life
  • Michael Arthur Long is the specific person the car is designed to protect
  • Michael has a new face and is legally dead, making him unrecognizable
  • There are still people who would kill Michael if they discovered he's alive
  • Michael was saved and brought to this location by Devon and others after being left to die
  • The car appears to be Michael's original vehicle but has been completely transformed
  • The vehicle can show off and has personality traits beyond basic programming
  • Michael initially wants to leave and return to his normal life
  • Devon mentions that Michael wouldn't have a life without their intervention
  • The car has advanced safety features that prevent harm even to people working underneath it
  • There are mysterious people operating "above the law" who are described as untouchable criminals
