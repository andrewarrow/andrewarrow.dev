Adams, Cuomo FLAME Zohran For Bench Press Failure

  • Eric Adams capitalized on the incident, tweeting about "a lifetime of hard work versus a silver spoon" and saying the politician can't handle "the weight of the job"
  • Andrew Cuomo also criticized the politician, saying "this guy can't bench his own body weight, let alone carry the weight of leading the most important city"
  • The incident has become part of a pattern of finding "silly scandals" for the politician, including attacks based on DSA platform positions he didn't personally endorse

  • The New York Times admitted their reporters had also witnessed cash exchanges from the Adams campaign but hadn't reported it previously
  • Cash bribes are considered unusually brazen corruption, as most political corruption involves "soft" benefits like free meals or travel
  • The socialist politician responded to the corruption scandal by organizing a citywide scavenger hunt as a tongue-in-cheek reference
  • The scavenger hunt announcement featured the politician holding the same type of chip bag used in the bribery attempt
  • Thousands of people participated in the scavenger hunt across New York City
  • The scavenger hunt was described as both "millennial cringe" and a positive way to get people outside and exploring the city
  • Eric Adams has multiple aids under investigation or indicted, representing "old school" big city machine-level corruption
  • The campaign worker involved in the bribery had previously been under FBI investigation
  • The discussion touched on how modern political corruption usually avoids direct cash exchanges in favor of less traceable benefits
