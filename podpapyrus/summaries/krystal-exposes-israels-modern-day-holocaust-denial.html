Krystal EXPOSES Israel's Modern Day Holocaust Denial

Key Points

  • UN experts with the IPC officially declared Gaza City in a state of man-made famine, only the fourth such declaration in the organization's history
  • Gaza City has reached famine metrics with 35% of households at catastrophic food insecurity levels and 55% at emergency levels
  • Other Gaza Strip regions are on track for famine without immediate ceasefire and aid surge
  • IPC report warns that without ceasefire and restored services, "avoidable deaths will increase exponentially"
  • Israel immediately launched efforts to deny the famine assessment's validity through propaganda campaigns
  • Israel falsely claimed IPC lowered malnutrition standards from 30% to 15% specifically to target Israel
  • The same 15% MUAC (mid-upper arm circumference) metric was used for declaring famine in Sudan in the previous year
  • Two valid assessment methods exist: weight-for-height (30% threshold) and MUAC (15% threshold) with different but equivalent standards
  • MUAC is often used when assessors have limited access, as in Gaza due to healthcare system destruction
  • MUAC is actually a more stringent criteria - 15% MUAC malnutrition typically corresponds to over 30% weight-for-height malnutrition
  • Gaza City did breach the 15% threshold in the second half of July, showing worsening conditions
  • Israel falsely claims Gaza hasn't met death thresholds by only counting direct starvation deaths, not famine-related deaths
  • IPC standards count all famine-related deaths including those from weakened immune systems, infections, and lack of medical care
  • Israeli Diaspora Affairs Ministry sponsored influencer trips to massacre sites to counter famine reports
  • Videos showing Gaza food markets are often old footage or from elsewhere, and prove nothing about overall conditions
  • These propaganda tactics mirror Nazi Holocaust denial methods used in Warsaw ghetto restaurants
  • Gaza hospitals show horrific malnutrition conditions with children suffering severe physical deterioration
  • Specific cases include infants like Amar and Sham who are starving due to lack of formula and proper nutrition
