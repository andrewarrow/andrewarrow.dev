Love at First Mic!

Audio-Only Dating Show

How It Works

From a browser you will login to a zoom style video call that is being recorded between you and your potential match. We the audience get to see both sides audio and video. But you "in the pod" will only hear audio.

Your camera needs to be on though, and you should be camera ready because that's the whole point of the show. You should have a nice quiet place to broadcast from (your own apartment, house, office).

What You Need

  • A good microphone is required
  • A quiet, private space to broadcast from
  • Real background - make your bed or clean up your kitchen
  • Camera ready appearance
  • Stable internet connection
  • Modern web browser

Disclaimer: We are not associated with the show "Love is Blind" in any way. We are calling this version "Love at First Mic" and need some people willing to try it.
